About Us (Fulch Hairs)

Welcome to Fulch Hairs, your ultimate destination for luxurious and high-quality female hair extensions. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and empowered in her own unique way. That's why we've made it our mission to provide you with the finest hair products that not only enhance your natural beauty but also make you stand out from the crowd.


Our Commitment to Quality

At Fulch Hairs, quality is our top priority. We meticulously source and select only the finest hair, ensuring that it meets the highest standards in terms of texture, thickness, and longevity. Our products are expertly crafted to give you a seamless and natural look, whether you're going for a glamorous evening style or an everyday, casual look. With our hair extensions, you can easily transform your appearance and boost your confidence.


Variety to Suit Your Style

We understand that every woman has her own unique style, which is why we offer a wide range of hair textures, lengths, and colors to choose from. Whether you prefer sleek and straight, bouncy curls, or natural waves, we have the perfect hair extensions to match your style and personality. Our extensive selection allows you to experiment with your look and express your individuality


Ethical and Sustainable Practices

Fulch Hairs is committed to ethical and sustainable practices. We source our hair from reputable suppliers who adhere to ethical standards, ensuring that our products are cruelty-free and produced with respect for both people and the environment. We are dedicated to making responsible choices at every step of our production process.

Meet Our CEO:


Miss. Faith Oluchi Okereke

Chief Executive Officer

Behind Fulch Hair, you'll find the visionary leadership of our CEO, Faith Okereke, a passionate entrepreneur based in the vibrant city of Port Harcourt, River State, Nigeria. Faith is not just a leader; she is a trailblazer who has carved her path in the beauty industry. Her journey began with a vision to empower women to embrace their beauty with confidence.

Born and raised in Nigeria, Faith has always had a deep connection to her roots and culture. She saw an opportunity to create a brand that not only celebrates the natural beauty of African women but also caters to the diverse beauty needs of women worldwide. Faith is driven by a commitment to excellence, a passion for quality, and a strong desire to make a positive impact on the beauty industry.

Under Faith's guidance, Faulch Hairs has grown from a local enterprise to a global brand, known for its exceptional quality and dedication to customer satisfaction. Her leadership has set a standard of excellence that extends to every aspect of our business, from product sourcing to customer service.

Thank you for choosing Fulch Hairs. We invite you to explore our exquisite collection of hair extensions and experience the transformation that quality hair can bring to your life. Join us on this journey of beauty, confidence, and empowerment. Elevate Your Beauty with Fulch Hairs.
